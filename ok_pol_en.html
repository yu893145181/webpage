What information is collected and how is it used?

 

Categories of Information Collected

 

In order to provide you with our services, we may ask you to provide the personal information necessary to provide you with the services. If you do not provide your personal information, we may not be able to provide you with our products or services.

 

We collect the following types of information (whether or not it is personal information):

 

About you: We may collect and use your profile picture, personal signature, nickname, gender, time zone, language, your sports preferences, and regional data.

 

Personal physical data: After launching OKSENWA, we will ask you to provide your birthday, height, weight, and gender.

 

Device Recorded Data: When you use OKSENWA to sync data from related devices, we will record your personal information, including activity information, sleep information, heart rate information, blood oxygen saturation (SpO2), weight, impedance value, distance, swimming style, pace, and exercise time.

 

Behavior Tagging Information: When you choose to tag related behaviors, OKSENWA will collect, including but not limited to, activity information and heart rate data to provide you with the corresponding service tagging features.

 

Call Information: When you use the call and text message notification features, OKSENWA will obtain data related to incoming calls, text messages, and your address book. With your permission, this data will be displayed on devices such as wristbands and watches. However, OKSENWA will not store your call, text message, or address book data.

 

Mobile phone information: When you use OKSENWA, we need to obtain your mobile phone operating system version, mobile phone brand and model, mobile phone hardware identifiers (such as IDFA, IMEI), mobile phone system time, and mobile phone alarms.

 

Network usage data: We may collect information such as network type and network signal related to specific OKSENWA features.

 

Music Control Information: When you use the music control feature, we will collect music information from your phone (e.g., song title, song playback status, volume) and sync it to your device. This information is only displayed on your device and is not stored.

 

Location Data: When you use location-based services or features, we may collect your location data.

 

Feedback Information: When you submit feedback, we will collect relevant user feedback logs, contact information, and other information based on your choices.

 

Device Information: When you use OKSENWA to connect to a hardware device, data including but not limited to the device's unique identifier, firmware version, approximate geographic location (non-personal data), and related feature usage statistics (non-personal data) will be transmitted to the server. This data collection may also apply to system or software updates, factory resets, etc.

 

Information you actively submit when using related features: If you set goals and heart rate reminders in OKSENWA, your exercise goals, weight goals, and heart rate reminder values ​​will be collected so that we can provide you with reminders or alerts when your goals are reached. If you choose to use the Women's Health feature, the information you proactively provide about the duration of your menstrual period, the number of days between the start of your two menstrual periods, the start date of your most recent menstrual period, and the menstrual start and end dates, as well as your physical condition and mood during your menstrual period, will be collected. If you choose to create your own watch face, any images you upload will be collected and used as your watch face background.

 

Storage permissions: Read and write local storage permissions. To ensure the security of the music and e-book functions, as well as your account and to save and store your current exercise data, read and write local storage permissions are required. This includes the ability to modify/delete the contents of the SD card and read the contents of the SD card.

 

 

 

To ensure your normal use of our services, maintain the normal operation of OKSENWA's basic functions, optimize product performance based on your device terminal and network status, improve your user experience and protect your account security, we will collect your device name, device type, device model and version, operating system, system properties, IP address, operator information, Wi-Fi status/parameters, device identifiers (such as IMEI, IMSI, MAC address, Android ID, etc.), class="s1">, hardware serial number), application ID, network type, sensor information, etc. Please note that individual sensor information does not constitute personal information, and we will not use sensor information for other purposes.

 

 

 

AutoNavi Maps and AutoNavi Positioning: This requires your location permission and will collect your device information, network status, location information, Wi-Fi information, device MAC address, software installation list, Android ID, sensor information, and device serial number to provide you with map and positioning services during outdoor activities.

The purpose of collecting personal information is to provide you with products and/or services and to ensure our compliance with applicable laws. We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

 

Calculating exercise and health results. We use personal physical data and/or data recorded by your device to calculate exercise and health results for display within the app and/or your device, such as: BMI, PAI, muscle mass, body fat, water content, protein, basal metabolic rate, visceral fat, bone mass, body type, age, stress levels, calorie consumption, maximum oxygen uptake, training effect, training load, recovery time, and blood oxygen saturation.

 

Physical Condition Analysis. Based on the personal data you provide and the data recorded by your device, we provide you with analysis related to your physical condition for your reference. For example, we provide you with an ideal weight range for your reference; we display your current and/or daily stress levels and/or stress trends.

 

Provide OKSENWA services. After syncing the relevant device with OKSENWA, the data recorded on the device will be synced to OKSENWA and displayed to the user.

 

Display caller information. When you receive a call or text message, some devices that support this feature will display the caller or message details.

 

Display music information. When you use the music control feature, some devices that support this feature will display music information (e.g., song title, song playback status, volume).

 

Determine if your phone is supported. Information related to your phone will be used to determine if your phone can use OKSENWA.

 

Alarm reminder function: We will collect your phone's alarm information and promptly alert you by vibrating your device when you set your alarm.

 

Bluetooth photography: You can control your phone's photography through some devices that support this feature. To do this, your device will pair with your phone via Bluetooth for photography control. During the Bluetooth pairing process, you will request Bluetooth permission from your phone.

 

Female health function: If you choose to use the female health function, you can record and view menstrual period information on some supported devices or on OKSENWA. We will predict your menstrual period and provide reminders based on the information you provide. We may also recommend appropriate training courses based on your menstrual cycle schedule and your physical condition during menstruation.

 

Displaying sleep information: We will use your personal data and device-recorded data to calculate and analyze your sleep status for display within OKSENWA, including sleep score, sleep duration, rapid eye movement (REM) sleep stages, sleep duration distribution, and sleep breathing quality.

 

Provide heart health monitoring: Some devices support heart health monitoring. If you choose to enable this feature, we may collect your heart rate information to display your heart rate status. We will notify you of changes in your data through your device and display them on your device and within OKSENWA.

 

Provide network-related services. OKSENWA will inform users of the data required to download exercise-related videos and determine whether a new version of OKSENWAis available based on different network types, network signal strength, and other information.

 

Statistical Analysis: We conduct statistical analysis of our services or devices and may share these statistics with the public or third parties for purposes such as demonstrating usage trends of our products or services, optimizing our products and services, and displaying rankings and friend comparisons. For example, we may use your step count to show you comparisons with users in your region. However, these statistics do not contain any personally identifiable information.

 

When you connect to our wearable devices, to ensure Bluetooth connection stability and timely message push, we need to use the auto-start service to ensure proper Bluetooth reconnection and app launch. Disabling the auto-start feature may result in Bluetooth reconnection failure and message push failures to the wearable device. You can disable the auto-start feature in Settings > Apps > Auto-start Management.
