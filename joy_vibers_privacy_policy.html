The JoyR+ smart ring collects and processes data related to health and wellbeing, such as heart rate, sleep quality, step count, and blood oxygen saturation, for the purpose of monitoring the user's health and wellbeing.

2. Legal Basis

The legal basis for processing your personal data is your consent (GDPR Article 6.1 a and 9.2 a). Consent is active, informed, and voluntary, and you can only withdraw it via email to info@joyearbuds.com.

3. Data Collected and Purposes of Use

Data Category Examples of Data Collected
Health and Biometric Data Heart rate, blood oxygen saturation, stress level
Sleep and Rest Data Sleep duration, quality, stages
Activity Data Step count, calorie consumption, movement
Device Technical Data Identifier, software version
User Data Name, email address, age, gender (optional)
Usage and Analytics Data Application usage data

Purposes of Use: Creating personalized health reports, device optimization, technical support, and development.

4. Data Transfer Outside the EU/EEA

We transfer data outside the EU/EEA. Risks associated with data partially transferred to servers outside the EU/EEA include:

  • Authorities in third countries may gain access to the data under local legislation.
  • Data protection practices may differ from EU requirements.
  • Exercising your rights may be more difficult.

The transfer of your data is necessary for the provision of cloud services.

5. Retention Period

  • During Active Use: As long as you use the service.
  • After Account Closure: Deletion within 30 days of your request.
  • Automatic Deletion: After 3 years of inactivity.

6. User Rights

You can send an email to info@joyearbuds.com to:

  • Request access to your data.
  • Get correction of errors.
  • Request the erasure of your data.
  • Restrict processing.
  • Object to processing.
  • Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

We will respond within 30 days.
