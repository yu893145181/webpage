Qore 2 – FAQs

Pebble Halo App

1What is Qore 2?
Qore 2 is a smart wearable designed to track health and fitness activities and notify you of calls and alerts through vibration.
2What is the Pebble Halo App used for?
The Pebble Halo App is used to connect Qore 2 with your smartphone, sync health and activity data, and manage device settings.
3Does Qore 2 have a display?
No, Qore 2 does not have a display. All alerts are provided through vibration, and data can be viewed in the Pebble Halo App.
4How does Qore 2 notify me of calls?
Qore 2 notifies you of incoming calls using vibration alerts.
5Which smartphones are compatible with Qore 2?
Qore 2 is compatible with Android and iOS smartphones that support Bluetooth and meet the minimum OS requirements.
6How do I pair Qore 2 with the Pebble Halo App?
Turn on Bluetooth, open the Pebble Halo App, tap "Add Device," and select Qore 2 to complete pairing.
7Does Qore 2 require Bluetooth to work?
Yes, Bluetooth is required to sync data and receive call and notification alerts.
8What health features are available on Qore 2?
Qore 2 supports heart rate monitoring, SpO₂ tracking, sleep monitoring, step counting, calorie tracking, and HRV.
9Can Qore 2 monitor heart rate continuously?
Yes, Qore 2 supports continuous heart rate monitoring for wellness tracking.
10Does Qore 2 track sleep automatically 24 * 7?
Yes, Qore 2 automatically tracks sleep 24 * 7, and detailed data can be viewed in the Pebble Halo App.
11Is SpO₂ monitoring available on Qore 2?
Yes, SpO₂ monitoring is supported and can be checked through the app.
12How accurate are the health readings?
Health data is intended for general fitness and wellness purposes only and should not be used for medical diagnosis.
13Does Qore 2 support sports modes?
Yes, Qore 2 supports multiple activity and sports tracking modes through the app.
14Will Qore 2 store data if my phone is not nearby?
Yes, Qore 2 stores data temporarily and syncs it once connected to the app.
15Does Qore 2 support firmware updates?
Yes, firmware updates (if available) can be installed through the Pebble Halo App.
16Is Qore 2 water resistant?
Yes, Qore 2 is water resistant and suitable for daily use. Avoid hot water and deep submersion.
17Does the Pebble Halo App need internet access?
Internet access is required for updates and cloud sync, but Bluetooth features work without internet.
18Is my personal data safe on the Pebble Halo App?
Yes, the app follows standard data privacy and security practices.
19How long does the Qore 2 battery last?
Battery life varies depending on usage, vibration alerts, and health tracking features. With normal usage it can easily last for 45 Days.
20Can Qore 2 be used without the Pebble Halo App?
Basic tracking works on Qore 2, but the Pebble Halo App is required to view data and manage settings.
21How do I reset Qore 2?
Qore 2 can be reset through the device system settings in the Pebble Halo App.
22What should I do if Qore 2 is not vibrating for call notifications?
Ensure Bluetooth is connected, call notification permissions are enabled.
23How do I reset Qore 2?
Qore 2 can be reset through the device system settings in the Pebble Halo App.
24What should I do if Qore 2 is not vibrating for call notifications?
Ensure Bluetooth is connected, call notification permissions are enabled.
