Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LE Band ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, password, and user profile information
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, Bluetooth connection data, and device settings
  • Health Data: Steps, calories, distance, sleep data, heart rate, blood glucose readings, and other fitness metrics
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for activity tracking and route mapping
  • Contact Information: Phone contacts for device synchronization (with your permission)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and performance data
  • Technical Data: Device model, operating system, app version, and crash reports
  • Network Information: IP address, connection type, and network performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Device Management: Connect, sync, and manage your LE Band device
  • Health Tracking: Monitor and analyze your fitness and health metrics
  • Data Synchronization: Sync data with Apple Health and other health platforms
  • User Experience: Personalize app features and improve functionality
  • Account Management: Create and maintain your user account

3.2 Secondary Uses

  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app performance
  • Customer Support: Provide technical assistance and respond to inquiries
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and legal obligations
  • Security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

We may share your information with:

  • Apple Health: With your explicit consent for health data synchronization
  • Cloud Storage Providers: For secure data backup and synchronization
  • Analytics Services: For app performance monitoring and improvement
  • Customer Support Tools: For providing technical assistance

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by:

  • Law Enforcement: In response to valid legal requests
  • Regulatory Authorities: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Court Orders: When ordered by a court of competent jurisdiction

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication procedures
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training for staff

5.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

6.2 Privacy Settings

You can control your privacy through:

  • App Permissions: Manage camera, location, contacts, and health data access
  • Notification Settings: Control push notifications and alerts
  • Data Sharing: Choose what data to sync with Apple Health
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and associated data

6.3 Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of:

  • Marketing Communications: Unsubscribe from promotional emails
  • Analytics: Disable analytics data collection
  • Location Tracking: Disable GPS location services

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • In-App Notification: Displaying a notice within the app
  • Email Notification: Sending an email to your registered address
  • Website Update: Posting the updated policy on our website
