Fire Lens AI– Delete User and Data Guide

How to Delete Your Account and Data

  1. Open the Fire Lens AI APP.
  2. Go to My > User Profile.
  3. Select Cancel Account.
  4. A warning pop-up window will appear.
  5. Confirm to log out and delete your account.

Important Notes

When you cancel your account: 

  • All your account information will be immediately deleted, including your email, password, personal details).
