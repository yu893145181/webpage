The Information We Collect

When you use Cellularline Orion to synchronize data from related devices, we record personal information (birthday, height, weight, gender), activity information (steps, calories), sleep (sleep length, sleep quality), heart rate (recent heart rate, average heart rate), blood oxygen saturation (SpO2, recent blood oxygen, blood oxygen range), temperature (maximum temperature, lowest temperature, average temperature), body weight, pace, exercise time. When you uninstall the application or change your device, it will result in the loss of sports health data, but this will not affect your local use of the sports health data recording function.

Behavior Labelling Information:

When you choose to label related behaviors, Cellularline Orion will collect activity information and heart rate data to provide you with corresponding service labelling function.

File Information:

When you connect our device, the device needs to update the version or font. With your permission, the device upgrade file, the font upgrade file will be downloaded. The pictures you need to share are cached and the local pictures are chosen when changing the avatar.

Mobile Phone Information:

When you use Cellularline Orion, we need to obtain your mobile phone operating system version, mobile phone brand model, mobile phone hardware identifier (IDFA/ IMEI/ IMSI/ UUID/ MAC), and mobile phone system time to meet the matching device function, incoming call function, push function and abnormal problem analysis needs.

Network Usage Data:

We may collect network type (2G/ 3G/ 4G/ 5G/ WIFI) and network signal information related to specific functions of Cellularline Orion.

Mobile Phone Software Installation List Information:

We may obtain your software installation list information. With your permission, we will synchronize your health data to the third-party software you choose for display or third-party software information to the device for display.

Music Control Information:

When you use the music control function, we will collect the music information (song name, album name, singer, play theme, song status) from your phone and synchronize it to the device. This information is only displayed on the device, and we will not store the aforementioned information.

Location Information:

When you use location-based program services or functions, we may collect your location information (weather data, since Android 6.0, App requires positioning permission to scan Bluetooth devices, use GPS to record your trajectory in movement).

Feedback:

When you submit relevant feedback, the relevant user feedback log, contact information and user name information will be collected based on your choice.

Equipment Information:

When you use Cellularline Orion to connect to our hardware device, the device unique identifier Mac address, firmware version, approximate geographic location data (non-personal data) and related function usage statistics (non-personal data) will be transmitted to the server. The above data collection may also apply to your updated system or software and reset.

The Information You Submit Proactively When Using the Relevant Functions:

You have set goals and heart rate reminders in Cellularline Orion, and your self-filled exercise goals and weight targets, and heart rate reminders will be collected so that you will be prompted or warned when the goals are reached. If you choose to use the female health function, the number of days of menstruation that you voluntarily fill in, the number of days between the start of two menstruations, the start date of the most recent menstruation, the start date of menstruation, the ending date, the physical condition and mood during the menstruation submitted each time during the use of this function will be collected; you can choose to make your own dial, and the pictures you upload will be collected by us as your dial background.

Sensor Information:

When using the sensor, you can directly turn on the indoor motion mode of the phone in Cellularline Orion and display your exercise results.

Information Provided by the Third-Party Service:

If you connect your account on the service to an account on another service, we will receive information from other services. If you connect to your Google account, we will receive information such as your name, profile photo, age range, language, email address and friend list. You can also authorize us to access your exercise or activity data from other services. You can also unauthorize our access to other services and stop sharing information on other services.

The Way We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes.

  • To set up a Cellularline Orion account: When setting up an account through Cellularline Orion, the information filled in will be used to set up your Cellularline Orion account and display the relevant data in Cellularline Orion.
  • To calculate or record exercise and health results: We use personal body data or device recorded data to calculate exercise and health results, which are used to show to you in the APP or device: BMI, steps, exercise trajectory, calorie consumption, heart rate data, sleep data, temperature data, weight data.
  • Physical condition analysis: Based on the personal body data you provide and the data recorded by the device, we provide you with the analysis related to your physical condition for your reference. For example: to provide you with an ideal weight range for your reference.
  • Health data: In order to calculate health data more accurately, it is necessary to collect the user's following information: personal name, birthday, gender, and personal phone number (sensitive information). If you refuse to provide this information, use the default values, which will affect the calculation accuracy of your health data.
  • To provide sports services: After the related device is synchronized with Cellularline Orion, the data recorded in the device will be synchronized to Cellularline Orion and displayed to the user.
  • Location services: When you allow us to collect accurate or ambiguous location information, we need the information to improve the function of the service, record sports locations or draw sports route maps, location-based weather services, and when you use BLE devices to scan, connect and record sports, we will need this information to obtain your device location authorization and collect the location information provided by you.
  • To display music information: When you use the music control function, some devices that support this function will display music information (song status, volume).
  • Photo scan code function: You can use some devices that support this function to control the phone's taking pictures, and use this additional function to complete the functions of taking pictures and scanning codes. When you use your device camera to scan the QR code and take photos, we need to access the relevant permissions of your device camera.
  • To provide female health function: When you choose to use this function, you can record and view menstruation-related information on some devices that support this function or in Cellularline Orion; we will predict your period and remind you based on the information filled in.
  • To display sleep information: We will use personal body data and device recorded data to calculate and analyze your sleep quality, which can be used to show to you in Cellularline Orion, including sleep time, sleep length distribution and sleep quality.
  • To provide network-related services: Cellularline Orion will prompt users whether a new version of Cellularline Orion is available to update according to different network types (2G/ 3G/ 4G/ 5G/ WIFI) and network signal information.
  • Statistical analysis: We conduct a statistical analysis of our services or device conditions, and may share these statistics with the public or third parties. The data are used to reveal the usage trends of our products or services, optimize our products and services, and show you the list of people you care about, but these statistics do not contain any of your identity information.
  • To provide and maintain services: By using the information we collect, we can provide services to you and abide by our terms of service contract with you. We need to use your information to provide you with Cellularline Orion control panels to track your exercises, activities and other tendencies; to achieve community functions of services; and to support you.
  • Analysis and statistics: We need to collect your device information, system basic information, device usage information, system usage information and the worn device usage information, including the pages and the buttons you click in the application, the functional operation of applications and device, which is used to analyze and calculate the use of device and applications.

Your Right to Access and Control Your Personal Information

Access Personal Information

In the application Cellularline Orion, you can access your exercise and health data through data cards, including step cards, exercise cards, sleep cards, heart rate cards, weight cards, blood oxygen cards, and device pages.

You can view your nickname, profile photo, gender, date of birth, height, weight, BMI and my emblem on the personal information page.

Update Personal Information

You can modify and update your nickname, profile photo, gender, date of birth, height and weight on the personal information page, where data obtained automatically from your mobile phone or sports health device is not available for editing and updating.

Delete Personal Information

By unpairing, you can clear information about paired devices.
Specific path: Setting → Device → Unpairing

You can also clear all sports health and other service data bound to your account by logging out of your account.
Specific path: Setting → Account Center → Cancelling the Account

Change the Scope of Your Authorized Consent

Each business function requires some basic personal information to be completed. You may give or withdraw your authorization at any time for the collection and use of additional personal information.

If you want to change the authorization of relevant permissions (location, address book, camera, album, microphone, storage, phone, SMS, pedometer, battery-optimized whitelist, reading notification), you can do so in the permission settings interface in app.
Specific path: Setting → Help → Permission Settings

You may also permanently withdraw our authority to continue to collect your personal information by logging out of your account. If you encounter operational problems during this process, you can contact us through the contact information provided at the end of this Privacy Policy. When you withdraw your consent, we will no longer handle the corresponding personal information. However, your decision to withdraw your consent will not affect the previous processing of personal information based on your authorization.
