ForOur – Delete User and Data Guide

How to Delete Your Account and Data

  1. Open the ForOur APP.
  2. Go to My > Profile.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. A warning pop-up window will appear.
  5. Confirm to log out and delete your account.

Important Notes

When you cancel your account:

  • All your account information will be immediately deleted, including your email, password, personal details, and data generated by your smart wearable devices (step count, heart rate, sleep, exercise, and training data).
